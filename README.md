### Description:

Want to be the very best? That no one ever was? Well look no further because with this mod you can be the ultimate Pokemon trainer!

## what this mod adds
- A new weapon combo : **Pokemon Trainer**
 This weapon combo is just an ordinary dagger and knife, however you have a nifty mark that allows you to catch enemies and release them to fight by your side!
Catching is luck based and can be improved with the selection of **augments** that you have at your disposal

- **Augments**, along with the weapon combo comes augments for it that do a variety of things. There are even accessory augments to help buff your pokemon even further.

- A **typing system**, it wouldn't be a Pokemon mod if the enemies didn't have types. Almost every enemy has either 1 or 2 types that define their attacks, some types are from the actual pokemon games, others well, I just made up to fit the game better. The types are as follows:
slime, undead, poison, plant, magic, stone, crystal, dark, flying, fire, steel
You can view the types of enemies on the bestiary 
There is a small **effectiveness system** which will  boost or weaken your Pokemons damage against certain enemies.

- **Relics**, Relics have been added to buff your pokemon through your perilous adventure in the dungeon

- **Milestones**, A few milestones to give some extra rewards and as a way to keep track of your progress as a pokemon trainer.
### Warning
- Ranged enemies/ ones that do not move, will do pretty much nothing unless you spawn them very close to your target, also if their projectiles are tracking projectiles, they will still lock onto you. So my suggestion, catch enemies that are mobile.
- Some relics like thundering gong will hurt your pokemon, so choose your relics with care
